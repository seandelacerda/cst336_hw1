Las Vegas Raiders

A Team of Firsts

First Native American Quarterback

Jim Plunkett won two superbowls for the Raiders after replacing the legendary Ken Stabler.

First African-American Head Coach of the Modern Era

Art Shell followed up his hall-of-fame playing career by coaching the Raiders over two separate stints.

First Latino Head Coach

Tom Flores played quarterback for the Raiders and later coached the team to two superbowl victories.

First Female CEO of an NFL Franchise

Amy Trask a.k.a. The Princess of Darkness.
